Neural networks rank among the most influential algorithms in [[AI]]. They drive breakthroughs in [[Computer Vision]], [[NLP]], [[Speech Recognition]] and countless applications, from forecasting to facial recognition. While today's [[DNN]] power complex systems such as [[Transformer]] and [[CNN]], the roots of neural networks trace back to simple models like linear regression and to the way the human brain processes, transforms and decides on incoming information.
