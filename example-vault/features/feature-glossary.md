Neural networks rank among the most influential algorithms in [[term-ai]]. They drive breakthroughs in [[term-computer-vision]], [[term-nlp]], [[term-speech-recognition]] and countless applications, from forecasting to facial recognition. While today's [[term-dnn]] power complex systems such as [[term-transformer]] and [[term-cnn]], the roots of neural networks trace back to simple models like linear regression and to the way the human brain processes, transforms and decides on incoming information.
